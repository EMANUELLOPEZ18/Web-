Performance and handling

The Vento Storm 300 features a 278 cc single-cylinder engine capable of producing around 26 horsepower. This power makes it ideal for smooth city rides, while also performing well on the highway. Its 6-speed manual transmission allows for better use of the engine, offering smooth acceleration and a good top speed without straining the motor. The inverted front suspension and rear monoshock provide stable and comfortable riding, effectively absorbing road imperfections. In addition, the front and rear disc brakes offer safe and precise stopping power. Overall, the Storm 300 delivers a well-balanced riding experience: agile in the city, solid on the highway, and with a sporty response that makes it fun to ride.
